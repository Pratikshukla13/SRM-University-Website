Our Campusues

SRM University Chennai is one of the top universities in India. Our Facilities

Central Library

SRM Library is nearly three decades old. The library consists of books, journals, e-journals and other resources in various branches and is equipped with reading halls on all the floors. The central library is automated with RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) technology, Centralized Air Conditioned (AC), and well protected with CCTV Security systems and fire alarms.

Hostels

SRM Chennai provides comfortable housing facilities for all students. The university has separate hostels for girls, boys and international students.

Canteens

SRMIST Canteens are located on different campuses in Chennai and offer a variety of foods, snacks and beverages at a minimal cost.
